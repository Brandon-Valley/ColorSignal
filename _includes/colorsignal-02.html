How to Play

A fast color-reading party game. Every round, one player becomes the Signal Giver.

1
See a secret shadeThe Signal Giver receives one target square that nobody else can see.
2
Give a one-word signalEveryone drags across the grid to preview a shade, then confirms their choice.
3
Give a two-word signalThe Signal Giver sees the first confirmed markers, gives a more specific clue, and players confirm one more shade.
4
Reveal and scoreExact target: 3 points. One square away: 2. Two squares away: 1. The Signal Giver scores for close guesses.
